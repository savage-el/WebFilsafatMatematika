Jeno NCT

Lee Je-no (Korean: 이제노, born April 23, 2000) is a South Korean singer, rapper, dancer, and songwriter under SM Entertainment. He is a member of the boy band NCT and its sub-unit NCT Dream.

Jeno was born in Incheon, South Korea, on April 23, 2000, and grew up in Ilsandong-gu, Goyang-si, Gyeonggi. From a young age, Jeno appeared in several advertisements and became accustomed to performing on television. He attended Hyunsan Elementary School, then Hyunsan Middle School, and later the School of Performing Arts Seoul, where he majored in dance before deciding to focus on a career in entertainment.

In 2014, Jeno appeared on the show Exo 90:2014 alongside Hansol, Johnny, Taeyong, Yuta, Ten, Jaehyun, Mark, Haechan, Jaemin, and Jisung. The following year, Jeno, Jaemin, Jisung, Mark, and Haechan participated in The Mickey Mouse Club on Disney Channel Korea. He officially debuted with NCT Dream in 2016 with the song "Chewing Gum."
